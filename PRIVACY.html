Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the Scanium mobile application.

1. Introduction

Scanium (“we”, “our”, “the app”) respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is processed.

Contact: contact@scanium.app (replace with your preferred support email if different)

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera and Images

Scanium uses your device’s camera to allow you to scan physical items.

  • Images are captured only when you actively use the scan feature.
  • Images are used solely to identify and analyze items.
  • Images are not used for advertising.

Depending on the feature used, processing may happen on the device, or images may be sent securely to Scanium’s backend services for analysis. Some analyses may involve third-party services (see section 4).

2.2 User-Provided Data

Scanium may store information you explicitly provide, such as:

  • Item names
  • Descriptions
  • Prices
  • Item condition (e.g., new, as good as new, used, refurbished)

This data is stored so you can manage your scanned items.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

Scanium may collect limited technical information necessary to operate the app, such as:

  • App version
  • Device type
  • Operating system version

This information is used only for stability, debugging, and performance purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate Scanium’s features
  • Analyze scanned items
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

We do not sell user data.

4. Third-Party Services

Scanium may use third-party services only to provide core functionality, such as image recognition. These services process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Examples may include cloud-based image analysis services. Scanium does not allow third-party services to use your data for advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

  • Scanned item data is retained as long as it is needed for app functionality.
  • You can remove items within the app.
  • We do not retain images longer than necessary for processing.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Scanium is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR), including access, correction, or deletion of your data. You can contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact: contact@scanium.app
