# Scanium Marketing Site

## Update the Google Play link
Edit `website/app.js` and replace the `PLAY_STORE_URL` constant with the final Play Store URL.

## Upload to Hostinger
1. Copy the contents of the `website/` folder (including `assets/`) to your Hostinger `public_html` directory.
2. Ensure `index.html`, `styles.css`, and `app.js` live at the root of `public_html`.
3. Upload the `assets/` folder alongside them so the logo and favicon load correctly.
