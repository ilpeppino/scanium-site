Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the Scanium mobile application.

1. Introduction

Scanium (“we”, “our”, “the app”) respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is processed.

Contact: contact@scanium.app (replace with your preferred support email if different)

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera and Images

Scanium uses your device’s camera to allow you to scan physical items.

  • Images are captured only when you actively use the scan feature.
  • Images are used solely to identify and analyze items.
  • Images are not used for advertising.

Depending on the feature used, processing may happen on the device, or images may be sent securely to Scanium’s backend services for analysis. Some analyses may involve third-party services (see section 4).

2.2 User-Provided Data

Scanium may store information you explicitly provide, such as:

  • Item names
  • Descriptions
  • Prices
  • Item condition (e.g., new, as good as new, used, refurbished)

This data is stored so you can manage your scanned items.

2.3 Account Information (Optional)

Scanium offers optional Google Sign-In to sync your data and enable personalized features. When you sign in with Google, we collect and store:

  • Your Google account ID (unique identifier)
  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile picture URL
  • Authentication session tokens

This information is used solely for authentication, data synchronization, and providing personalized features. We do not access any other Google account data.

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

Scanium may collect limited technical information necessary to operate the app, such as:

  • App version
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • IP address (for security and rate limiting)

This information is used only for stability, debugging, performance, and security purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate Scanium’s features
  • Analyze scanned items
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

We do not sell user data.

4. Third-Party Services

Scanium may use third-party services only to provide core functionality, such as image recognition. These services process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Examples may include cloud-based image analysis services. Scanium does not allow third-party services to use your data for advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

  • Scanned item data is retained as long as it is needed for app functionality.
  • You can remove items within the app.
  • We do not retain images longer than necessary for processing.
  • Account data (if you sign in with Google) is retained until you delete your account.

6. Account Deletion and Your Data

You have full control over your account and data. You can delete your account at any time through one of the following methods:

6.1 In-App Deletion

If you have the Scanium app installed, you can delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open the Scanium app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to the Account section (sign in required)
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your choice

6.2 Web-Based Deletion

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request account deletion via our web form:

Request Account Deletion

For security, we'll send a verification email to confirm the deletion request. The verification link expires after 1 hour.

6.3 What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your account profile (email, display name, profile picture)
  • All authentication sessions and tokens
  • eBay marketplace connections and stored OAuth tokens
  • All draft and published listings
  • Scan history and item data

6.4 Data Retention After Deletion

Some anonymized data may be retained for legal compliance, security, and fraud prevention purposes, including:

  • Aggregated usage statistics (not linked to your identity)
  • Security logs (IP addresses, anonymized identifiers)
  • Transaction records required by law

This retained data cannot be used to identify you and is stored according to legal requirements and industry standards.

6.5 Deletion Timeline

Account deletion is immediate and cannot be undone. All your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Scanium is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR), including access, correction, or deletion of your data. You can contact us to exercise these rights.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact: contact@scanium.app
