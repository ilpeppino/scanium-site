# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-01-05_

## 1. Introduction

Scanium (“we”, “our”, “the app”) respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the **Scanium** mobile application.

If you have any questions, you can contact us at:  
**Email:** scanium@gtemp1.com

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Camera and Images

Scanium uses your device’s **camera** to allow you to scan physical items.

- Images are captured **only when you actively use the scan feature**
- Images are used solely to identify and analyze items
- Images are **not used for advertising**

Depending on the feature used:

- Processing may happen **on the device**, or
- Images may be sent securely to Scanium’s backend services for analysis

Some analyses may involve third-party services (see section 4).

---

### 2.2 User-Provided Data

Scanium may store information you explicitly provide, such as:

- Item names
- Descriptions
- Prices
- Item condition (e.g. new, used, refurbished)

This data is stored so you can manage your scanned items.

---

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information

Scanium may collect limited technical information necessary to operate the app, such as:

- App version
- Device type
- Operating system version

This information is used **only** for stability, debugging, and performance purposes.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and operate Scanium’s features
- Analyze scanned items
- Improve app performance and reliability
- Fix bugs and technical issues

We do **not** sell user data.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Scanium may use third-party services **only to provide core functionality**, such as image recognition.

These services process data strictly according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Examples may include:

- Cloud-based image analysis services

Scanium does not allow third-party services to use your data for advertising purposes.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- Scanned item data is retained as long as it is needed for app functionality
- You can remove items within the app
- We do not retain images longer than necessary for processing

---

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Scanium is **not intended for children under the age of 13**.  
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR), including:

- Access to your data
- Correction or deletion of your data

You can contact us to exercise these rights.
